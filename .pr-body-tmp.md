## Summary
- Add per-table customer assistance QR (`/a/:token`) with Call waiter and live status polling.
- Broadcast `assistance_requested` on table WebSocket updates for staff UI.
- On checkout start, create/refresh bill share so the assistance page can show bill review + download.
- Staff endpoints: `GET /tables/:id/assistance-qr`, `POST /tables/:id/clear-assistance`.
